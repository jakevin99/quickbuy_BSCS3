How to Add Images to Your Site

To make your landing page look like the reference design, you need proper images:

  1. Download the image samples from the links below
  2. Save them to your static/images folder
  3. Make sure to use the correct filenames

2. Nike Air Force 1 Image

Important Notes:

  • Use royalty-free images for your project
  • Make sure images are properly sized (not too large)
  • The hero image should be landscape orientation
